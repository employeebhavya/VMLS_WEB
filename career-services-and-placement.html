About Us

The Office of Career Services and Placements is committed to empowering students and alumni as they navigate their career paths. We understand that the transition from education to the workforce can be both exciting and daunting. We are here to provide comprehensive support that equips you with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to succeed in today’s competitive job market.

We at VMLS are dedicated to guide you through every step of your career journey. Whether you are just starting your professional exploration or looking to advance in your current field, we offer a range of services designed to meet your unique needs.

Our Services

Career Counselling

Engage in one-on-one sessions with our experienced counselors who will help you identify your career goals, develop strategies, and access valuable resources tailored to your aspirations.

Resume and Cover Letter Reviews

Receive personalized feedback on your application materials to ensure they effectively showcase your skills and experiences, making a strong impression on potential employers.

Interview Preparation

Participate in mock interviews and receive expert tips to enhance your performance during actual interviews. We focus on building your confidence and helping you articulate your strengths.

Networking Opportunities

Attend events that connect you with alumni and industry professionals, allowing you to build relationships that can lead to internships and job offers. Networking is a crucial part of career development, and we facilitate these connections.

Job Listings

Gain access to exclusive job postings and internship opportunities specifically tailored to your field of study. Our partnerships with various organizations ensure that you have access to a wide range of options.

Workshops and Seminars

Participate in regular programming covering various topics related to career development, including resume writing, interview techniques, and industry trends. These workshops are designed to enhance your skills and keep you informed about the job market.

We are passionate about helping you achieve your professional dreams. Our office is a welcoming space where you can explore possibilities, gain insights, and develop the necessary tools for a successful career. Whether you are seeking guidance on your first job or looking for ways to advance in your current role, we are here to support you every step of the way.
