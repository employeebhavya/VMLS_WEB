Dr. Anuradha Ganesan

Vice President

Dr. Anuradha Ganesan is a highly accomplished professional known for her dynamic leadership and extensive contributions across various domains. As the Vice President of Vinayaka Mission's Research Foundation, Deemed to be University, she excels in managing a diverse portfolio encompassing education, healthcare, infrastructure development, and community outreach initiatives.

Dr. Anuradha's journey in academia and healthcare began with her attainment of a prestigious medical degree from Kasturba Medical College, Manipal. Leveraging her medical background, she adeptly oversees both health and non-health faculties at VMRF-DU, ensuring a holistic approach to education and research. Her influence also extends to the healthcare sector, where she plays a pivotal role in the administration of Vinayaka Missions Institute of Medical Sciences (VIMS), a renowned 200-bed super-speciality hospital located in Salem. Under her guidance, VIMS continues to provide exceptional healthcare services and foster groundbreaking medical research.

Driven by a passion for innovation and entrepreneurship, Dr. Anuradha spearheads the Ganesan Incubation and Entrepreneurship Center (GIEC), empowering aspiring entrepreneurs to realize their visions and contribute to economic growth. In her latest endeavour, Dr. Anuradha is leading the establishment of a state-of-the-art 350-bed super-speciality hospital in Puducherry, slated to commence operations in April 2024. This initiative underscores her commitment to expanding access to high-quality healthcare and driving positive change in underserved communities.

Beyond her professional commitments, Dr. Anuradha is deeply involved in social impact initiatives aimed at uplifting society and promoting education. She is the visionary founder of “Thirukkural Semmal Na. Manimozhiyanar Muthamizh Aayam”, is an organisation dedicated to enhancing Tamil literacy skills among college students. Additionally, Dr. Anuradha actively participates in philanthropic endeavours by engaging with Vinayaka Mission Academy, LBR Foundation India, and the Rotary Club of Madras. Her unwavering dedication to serving humanity and advancing knowledge exemplifies her as a true leader and changemaker in her field.
