Transformative LL.M. Program: Bridging Theory with Practice

Our LL.M. programme offers a unique blend of academic rigor, industry exposure, and innovative teaching methods designed to shape future leaders in law. Through collaborative group projects, hands-on capstone experiences, and interactive discussions, students tackle real-world legal and corporate challenges alongside industry leaders. Case studies, visual learning tools, and in-depth analysis of corporate scandals and market shifts enhance understanding of complex legal and financial concepts. Stay ahead in a rapidly evolving legal landscape by exploring emerging technologies like blockchain, AI, fintech, and algorithmic trading. With personalized dissertation guidance, insights from Tier 1 law firm partners, renowned in-house counsels, and leading arbitrators, this program ensures both academic excellence and practical, industry-relevant experience.

LL.M. IN
corporate and financial laws

Immerse yourself in the intricacies of corporate and financial law with a curriculum tailored to tackle the challenges of today’s business world. Explore Comparative Corporate Governance, Finance, and Policy, by delving into global issues in corporate law and regulatory frameworks. Master the art of Mergers and Acquisitions, gaining insights into deal structuring and negotiation strategies. Dive deep into Corporate Insolvency, understanding the legal mechanisms for restructuring and liquidation. With optional subjects like Capital Markets Regulation and Project Finance and Infrastructure Disputes, customize your learning to suit your career ambitions.

SEMESTER 1

Research Methods and Legal Writing 3 Credits

Law and Justice in a Globalized World 3 Credits

Comparative Public Law and Governance 3 Credits

Mergers and Acquisitions 2 Credits

Commercial Dispute Resolution

Develop expertise in resolving complex commercial conflicts with a curriculum designed to refine your litigation and arbitration skills. Optional subjects such as Mediation Law and Practice, Corporate Law Disputes and TMT Disputes allow you to specialize in niche areas requiring specialized knowledge, preparing you to excel in the ever-evolving field of dispute resolution.
