Report on Workshop: "Teaching and Learning Tools" - August 28, 2024

Vinayaka Mission Law School (VMLS) organized a workshop on "Teaching and Learning Tools" on August 28, 2024, as part of its ongoing efforts to enhance faculty development and innovation in education. The workshop was led by Dr. Vembanoor Nedumaran, Senior Consultant in Artificial Intelligence, who was invited as the guest speaker. Dr. A. Francis Julian delivered the keynote address, while Dean Dr. Ananth Padmanaban welcomed the participants.

The session focused on the integration of AI in educational practices, exploring how AI tools can optimize teaching methodologies and improve the overall learning experience for students. Dr. Nedumaran shared valuable insights on the practical challenges and potential benefits of AI in education, addressing the concerns raised by faculty members regarding AI's role in academic settings. He provided practical solutions to help educators effectively adopt AI tools in their teaching practices.

The interactive nature of the workshop fostered active engagement from participants, who discussed both the advantages and limitations of AI in education. The workshop concluded successfully, leaving faculty members more confident in their ability to utilize AI-enhanced teaching and learning tools to enrich the academic environment.

This workshop aligns with VMLS's commitment to innovative teaching and continuous professional development of its faculty members.
