Research: Importance & Application After Law School

The VMLS Research Committee successfully organized a guest lecture titled “Research: Importance & Application After Law School.” The session, led by Mr. Vignesh Ram Hariharan, was specifically designed for final-year LLB students and saw enthusiastic participation from students across various batches. The lecture provided valuable insights into the crucial role of research in legal practice post-graduation.

VMLS | 20 minutes ago | 1 min read

Lecture Highlights

Significance of Research in Legal Practice

Mr. Vignesh Ram Hariharan highlighted the indispensable role research plays in the legal profession. Key points discussed included:

  • Shaping Legal Arguments: Emphasized how thorough research underpins strong legal arguments.
  • Understanding Precedents: Discussed the importance of understanding and applying legal precedents.
  • Staying Updated: Stressed the need for continuous research to stay abreast of legal developments.

Practical Tips and Real-World Examples

Mr. Hariharan shared practical tips on conducting effective legal research, drawing from his own experiences to provide real-world examples. He demonstrated how systematic research can lead to better case outcomes and professional growth.

Identifying Research Problems

  • Spot relevant legal issues.
  • Formulate research questions.
  • Approach complex legal scenarios with a research-oriented mindset.

Conclusion

The guest lecture by Mr. Vignesh Ram Hariharan was an enriching experience for the final-year LLB students. It underscored the critical importance of research in legal practice and provided practical knowledge that the students can apply in their future careers. The session was interactive and engaging, with students gaining a deeper understanding of the practical applications of research post-law school.
