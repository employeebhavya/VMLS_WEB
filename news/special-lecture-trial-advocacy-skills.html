Special Lecture trial advocacy skills

Event Overview

The Global Tamil Law Centre (GTLC) at Vinayaka Mission’s Law School (VMLS) recently hosted an enlightening lecture on trial advocacy strategies within the courtroom, featuring the esteemed Dr. I. Jayanthi, District Judge from Egmore, Chennai. Held on April 24th, 2024, this event provided a unique and invaluable opportunity for legal enthusiasts and professionals to explore the intricate dynamics of constitutional law practice.

VMLS | 26th to 28th September 2024 | 1 min read

Speaker Profile

Dr. I. Jayanthi, with her extensive experience and expertise as a District Judge, brought a wealth of knowledge to the lecture. Her distinguished career and profound understanding of the legal system added significant depth to the discussion, making the session both engaging and highly informative.

Key Highlights

Focus Areas

The lecture centered around trial advocacy strategies, with Dr. Jayanthi shedding light on the nuanced tactics crucial for effective courtroom advocacy. Key points included:

  • Various strategies that can enhance the effectiveness of legal arguments and improve overall courtroom performance.
  • Insights into constitutional law practice, emphasizing practical applications and real-world scenarios.

Interactive Discussion

The session included a lively interactive component where participants engaged with Dr. Jayanthi, posing questions and discussing various aspects of trial advocacy and constitutional law. Attendees gained actionable takeaways that could be directly applied to enhance their legal practice and advocacy skills.

Participant Feedback

  • Engagement: The lecture attracted a diverse audience of students, faculty, and legal professionals, all of whom were highly engaged and appreciative of the content presented.
  • Learning Experience: Participants found Dr. Jayanthi’s insights particularly valuable, noting the practical relevance and clarity of her explanations.
  • Interactive Component: The interactive segment was well-received, allowing for a deeper exploration of specific queries and fostering a collaborative learning environment.

Conclusion

The lecture on trial advocacy strategies by Dr. I. Jayanthi was a resounding success, providing attendees with critical insights into effective courtroom advocacy and constitutional law practice. This event underscores the GTLC’s commitment to offering enriching educational experiences that empower and educate the legal fraternity.

We look forward to hosting more such valuable events that contribute to the academic and professional development of our community. Stay tuned for future initiatives from the Global Tamil Law Centre at VMLS.
