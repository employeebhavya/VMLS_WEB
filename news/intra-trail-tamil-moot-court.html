Intra-trail Tamil Moot Court

Event Overview

Participants and Rounds:

  • Teams: 19 teams participated, demonstrating their skills in legal argumentation and trial advocacy.
  • Judges:
    • Preliminary and Semi-Final Rounds: Judged by 10 district court advocates and 2 high court advocates.
    • Final Round: Presided over by a senior high court advocate, our esteemed Dean, Ananth Padmanabhan, and Prof. Krishna Kishore.
VMLS | 26th to 28th September 2024 | 1 min read

Key Highlights

Judging Panel

The preliminary and semi-final rounds were evaluated by a panel of experienced district and high court advocates, ensuring a rigorous and fair assessment. The final round was a prestigious affair, with adjudication by:

  • A senior high court advocate
  • Dean Ananth Padmanabhan
  • Prof. Krishna Kishore

This esteemed panel added immense value to the competition.

Organizational Excellence

The competition was flawlessly executed, thanks to the dedicated efforts of Dr. Fowmina C., our dedicated organizer. Her meticulous planning and unwavering commitment ensured the smooth running of the event, providing a seamless experience for all participants and attendees.

Reflection and Future Vision

As we celebrate this milestone, it is essential to reflect on the broader impact of legal advocacy in shaping society. This competition not only provided a platform for aspiring legal professionals to hone their skills but also highlighted the vital role of advocacy in advancing justice and championing excellence in the legal profession.

The Tamil Trial Advocacy competition at VMLS underscores our commitment to fostering a culture of excellence, rigorous academic pursuit, and practical experience in legal education. We look forward to hosting more such enriching events that empower our students and contribute to the advancement of justice in society.
