NSS Lake Cleaning Drive

The NSS (National Service Scheme) wing of Vinayaka Missions Law School, in association with the Tamil Nadu State Wetland Authority and Ooruni Foundation, organized a lake cleaning drive on 4-06-2023 and 5-06-2023. The event was conducted to commemorate World Environment Day and raise awareness about environmental conservation and waste management under the theme “Waste to Wealth.” The enthusiastic participation of the students made the event a resounding success.

VMLS | 20 minutes ago | 1 min read

A speech competition on the topic “Waste to Wealth” was the highlight of the event. Students took turns delivering insightful speeches, highlighting innovative approaches to waste management and the potential economic benefits associated with recycling and upcycling. Their passion and knowledge resonated with the audience, inspiring everyone to take action.

Simultaneously, students also showcased various artworks that were created from wastage, highlighting the potential of turning waste materials into useful products. After the speeches and art exhibition, it was time for the students to put their words into action. Equipped with gloves, bags, and a strong determination, they embarked on a mission to clean the lake and its surroundings. Working in teams, they scoured every nook and cranny, collecting litter and debris that had accumulated over time. Laughter and camaraderie filled the air as the students embraced the spirit of teamwork and shared purpose. Their collective efforts transformed the lake’s appearance, restoring its natural beauty.

Our Students Ms. Gowri and Mr. Rishi Kesavan were presented with the best speakers award for their informative and eloquent speech. Our joint efforts and shared vision created a conducive environment for raising awareness about environmental conservation. The lake cleaning drive highlighted the importance of partnerships in addressing environmental challenges and fostering sustainable practices.

As the day drew to a close, the impact of the event was palpable. The students had not only cleaned the lake but had also left a lasting impression on the minds of all who witnessed their dedication. The event served as a reminder of our responsibility to protect the environment and utilize waste as a valuable resource. The lake cleaning drive organized by the NSS wing of Vinayaka Missions Law School was a testament to the students’ commitment to environmental conservation and their ability to effect positive change.
