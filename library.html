Library

The VMLS Library stands as a beacon of modernity, seamlessly integrating traditional library services with cutting-edge technology. This report highlights the key facilities and features that make the VMLS Library a premier resource center.

The library boasts a diverse collection of books spanning various genres and disciplines, catering to a wide range of academic and personal interests. Technologically, it is equipped with advanced computer systems and high-speed internet access, offering patrons robust digital research tools and a comprehensive digital catalog. The digital library service includes access to JSTOR, HeinOnline, SCC Online, Manupatra, and more.

Architecturally, the library combines aesthetic appeal with functional design, providing spacious reading areas illuminated by ambient lighting that fosters a conducive study environment. The fully air-conditioned space ensures year-round comfort for all users.

Moreover, the VMLS Library serves as a vibrant community hub, regularly hosting workshops, author meet-and-greets, and book discussions to promote the exchange of ideas and collective learning.
